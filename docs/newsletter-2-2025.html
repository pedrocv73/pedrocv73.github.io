Newsletter 2/2025 — Decolonization as Fault Line

Date: 20 November 2025

Summary

Decolonization was rushed, silent, and without democratic transition, leaving a memory archived but never edited. The cabinet examines three axes: radicalization vs. military moderation, current rhetoric vs. institutional memory, and the Realpolitik that closed the file. Celebrating democracy requires acknowledging memory as an editorial choice — and understanding how the past still shapes the present.
