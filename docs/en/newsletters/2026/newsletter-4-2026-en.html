Newsletter 4/2026 — Reactive drive — when systems align under pressure

Date: 9 February 2026

Summary

Europe appears united, but its unity is reactive — driven by pressure, not conviction. History shows that systems held together by fear collapse once the stimulus fades. Real estate mirrors this dynamic: apparent stability sustained by external forces. Both Europe and the market function under moderate pressure — when it rises, structural fragility emerges.
