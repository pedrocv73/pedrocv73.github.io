Characters

The Pragmatist

Measures the political cost of every silence. Every sentence is a variable.

The Memory Keeper

Recalls dates and consequences: What is the objective? What is at stake? In the Crisis Cabinet, the archive neither lies nor omits.

The Impact Simulator

Projects possible futures. Reveals invisible fractures.

The Crisis Writer

Rejects adjectives without context and chooses the right moment to communicate. “Serious for whom, exactly?”

The Invisible Reader

Reads between the lines and understands the informed audience. Forces everyone to justify each choice.
