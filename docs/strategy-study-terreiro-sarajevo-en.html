Strategic Study — Terreiro do Paço (1908) / Sarajevo (1914)

Summary

A comparative analysis of the 1908 regicide in Lisbon and the 1914 assassination in Sarajevo, showing how both events expose the structural fragility of political regimes already under severe strain. The study highlights the role of social tensions, institutional erosion and the inability of political elites to recognise the depth of the crises preceding both collapses.

Full content available in Portuguese. English summary provided for context only.
