The Logic of a Crisis Cabinet

Summary

Thinking in crisis‑cabinet mode is an institutional skill Portugal has never cultivated. This essay argues for disciplined analysis — filtering noise, mapping risks, and building scenarios — and presents the Crisis Cabinet as a conceptual prototype for strategic maturity.
